Welcome to Our Page!

What is Konviv?

Konviv is for people who need more intelligence around how they manage their finances. We use an artificial intelligence categorization system to provide you better data about your spending and savings, and help optimize your financial behavior around your lifestyle!


See our website for more information:
www.konviv.com

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

Financial empowerment and inclusion for all.

Our Mission

Konviv is committed to working relentlessly to realize a future where ALL are financially empowered. We are committed to providing the most accessible, intuitive, inclusive and empowering experience for all our users so they can achieve financial freedom and through that freedom do better for themselves, their dreams, their family, their community and our world. And being global citizens ourselves, we are excited to build global financial solutions that work cross-borders and internationally.

Our goal is to empower a minimum of 1 billion people through our services and products.

Why Donate?

While we are actively seeking other funding routes, we need to quickly fundraise money to keep the lights on and pay our contractors. Deep has basically been working 3 jobs. Luz pulled all of her life retirement savings and has been living off of them, but they are quickly running out. Luz has also been Lyft driving on the side, but the amount is so small that it doesn’t justify the time commitment. This is go time, and all of our time really needs to be focused on building Konviv, which is why we are coming to you.

We are starting to institutional investors, but this process might take some time, and thus for the meantime we are looking to our network of family and friends. If there are other ways you want to help Konviv, scroll to our “Other ways you can help” section.

Where is the Money Going?

Primarily, the money is going towards paying our contract development team in Costa Rica who developed our Minimum Viable Product. It is also going towards the start of paying the salary of our first full-time engineers who will be building out the enterprise architecture of the Konviv platform so it can eventually scale to hundreds of millions of users. A smaller part of this first donation in funds will go to bills for Konviv's founders as they continue to seek wider institutional funding for a full pre-seed round. If you have any more questions, please reach out to luz@konviv.com
