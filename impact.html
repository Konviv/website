Our Vision


Within our lifetime, all individuals around the world will have a widely recognized financial identity with cutting-edge tools to be active participants and creators in the world economy.



Financial Inclusion for All is Indeed Within Our Reach.

Our Mission



Konviv is committed to working relentlessly to realize a future where ALL are financially empowered. We are committed to providing the most accessible, intuitive, inclusive and empowering experience for all our users so they can achieve financial freedom and through that freedom do better for themselves, their dreams, their family, their community and our world.

...financial technology is on the verge of transforming the lives of billions of people who were traditionally left out of the mainstream.

Konviv is committed to making sure these financial technologies are accessible by all and empowering to all.

Theory of Impact

We’re at the beginning of a tremendous opportunity to disrupt people’s struggles with their money while providing individuals an opportunity to access new forms of capital and grow their income using artificial intelligence and emerging digital tools.

At Konviv, we are building financial ecosystems for emerging markets, and providing the most accessible, intuitive, and empowering financial services and products.

While we are thinking globally, we are starting with communities in the U.S. where over 57% of households have less than $400 saved in case of emergencies.

This isn't just about finance.

Impact is at the heart of what we do.

We are committed to using tech to disrupt some of the most pressing economic development issues of our time. We’re committed to working directly with people to empower them to rise above any financial obstacles they may be facing. This commitment comes from our own lived experiences and direct knowledge and struggles with poverty.


We’re more than an app – we’re a company committed to meaningful long-lasting social change.
